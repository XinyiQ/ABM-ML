This is for ABM model.
This package contains the Netlogo scripts, the data used in and the final output of the ABM model.

Group5_ABM.nlogo is the Netlogo scripts.
Schier_shape.shp is the boundary of study area, was clipped from the given dataset.
TP2010.csv and TP2014.csv were the temperature and precipitation data obtained from RIVM, and were processed to get the period of study time. The three columns respective represent: temperature, precipitation, season(1-winter, 2-spring, 3-summer, 4-fall).
The hazard.ASC was resampled and refilled in ArcGIS to fit it to the landuse map of Schiermonnikoog.
The schier_ascii.asc is a raster map which contains four kinds of land cover type.
